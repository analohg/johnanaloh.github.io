I am a data professional currently based in London, United Kingdom. Over the last 4 years, I have worked at some of the most innovative technology startups in various capacities in the data lifecycle. I have worked as a Data Analyst, Product Analyst, Data Visualization Developer and Data Engineer.

In my spare time, I work on some pretty interesting projects, and I write around topics related to data. You can find more on the projects I have worked on here.
